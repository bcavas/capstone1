Ever STRONGER

since '16

History

Forged in 2016 when team CHOPAIN registered in its first official powerlifting meet, the name has since evolved from a gym to an ethos for living.

True to its roots as a brotherhood of lifters, CHOPAIN is first and foremost a family. Our fellow lifter's success is also our own and we put them, and their goals, first in everything that we do.

DEDICATION

This is not a hobby. Not even an obsession. This is who we are. And it's the commitment of a lifetime.

VISION

We walk the path less traveled. Our journey never ends. We are stronger everyday, until the day we die.

CREDO

Self-improvement is the worthiest pursuit in life and strength is the truest measure of betterment.

VALUES

Intensity in training. Consistency in everything. Humility in victory. Resolve in defeat.
